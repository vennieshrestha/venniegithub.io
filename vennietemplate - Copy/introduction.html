Introduction

Hello, my name is Vennie Ratna Shrestha, and I'm originally from Nepal(In case you're wondering where Nepal is, it's in southern Asia, between India and China; it's a separate nation unconnected to either India or China). This is my second semester as a freshman majoring in mass communication with a specialization in advertising and strategic media. I wouldn't call myself an introvert, but neither would I call myself an extrovert. I consider myself to be more of an outgoing person, but I hate crowds and avoid approaching people at any costs. That was a brief introduction to who I am. Moving on to my major, I specifically choose this degree because I want to work in the media, whether in front or behind the cameras. I believe the field of mass communication majors is pretty broad in terms of professions. Additionally, everyone agrees that technology will eventually rule the world, therefore it's advantageous to work in the media to at least have a basic understanding of it. I look forward to learning a lot from your class, sir, especially about how codings are used to design for digital media. I want to make the most of every opportunity I have in your class to brighten my future. I'll use every minute to the fullest. Thank you!!
